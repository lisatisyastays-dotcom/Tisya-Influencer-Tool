ERV model explained

Expected Return Value (ERV) converts influencer reach into a rupee figure comparable to what Tisya would spend on Meta ads for equivalent exposure. This makes barter a financial decision, not a social media decision.

ERV = (accounts reached × posts) ÷ 1,000 × ₹500
Barter viable when: ERV ≥ stay cost

₹500/1,000 reached = 2× Meta CPC (₹65) organic premium. Accounts trust, follower acquisition value, and brand association premium over cold ad clicks.

Standard outreach message

Copy and send this to every influencer or prospective collaborator when they reach out. Replace [Name] with their name. 

Hi [Name],

Thank you for your interest in collaborating with Tisya Stays! We're excited to explore this further.

To help us assess the right kind of collaboration for you, we need a few details upfront. Please share the following:

📊 PUBLIC PROFILE INFO
• Instagram handle
• Total followers
• Content niche (travel / lifestyle / fashion / other)

📱 INSTAGRAM INSIGHTS (only visible to you as the account owner)
Please send screenshots of Insights from your 3 most recent reels or posts. We specifically need:
• Accounts reached — the number of unique people who saw the post (not total views)
• Saves
• Follower vs Non-follower split (shown as a % breakdown in Insights)

How to find this: Open a post or reel → tap the "View Insights" or "See Insights" button below it → scroll down to see reach, saves, and audience breakdown. A screenshot of this page for 3 posts is all we need.

Once we have this, we'll come back to you promptly with what we can offer.

Looking forward to working together!

Best,
Tisya Stays Marketing Team
